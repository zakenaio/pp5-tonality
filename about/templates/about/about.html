Who we are

Anders Miller.

founder

In a small town, a young record collector, Anders, transformed his passion into a thriving hub. Surrounded by stacks of vinyl, he started "Tonality," a record store that quickly became a sanctuary for music lovers. Anders keen ear for rare finds and classics turned Tonality into a beloved community treasure.

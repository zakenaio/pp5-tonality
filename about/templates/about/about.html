About Tonality.

How it started.

Tonality was born from a shared passion for music and a frustration with the limitations of the mainstream industry. We envisioned a platform to share our love for rare and classic sounds, a space where music could truly resonate.

Anders "uFo", a record collector with an insatiable curiosity for hidden gems, found himself yearning for a deeper connection with music. Surrounded by vinyl stacks in his small town, Anders embarked on a mission to create a haven for music enthusiasts like himself.
